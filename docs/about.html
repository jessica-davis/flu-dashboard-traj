About This Dashboard

This dashboard displays weekly influenza hospitalization forecasts for U.S. states and territories, powered by the EpyStrain ensemble model. Forecasts are generated each week and include projections for 1–4 weeks ahead.

Dashboard Views

  • Influenza Trend — Whether hospitalizations are forecast to increase, decrease, or remain stable.
  • Influenza Activity — The projected level of influenza activity (low, moderate, high, very high).
  • Weekly Hospital Admissions — Estimated hospitalization counts, available as totals or per 100k population.

Uncertainty Estimates

Each forecast includes uncertainty estimates. Most Likely shows the median projection, while Lower End and Upper End reflect the 10th and 90th percentiles of the forecast distribution.

Trajectory Forecasts

The trajectory chart shows individual model simulations for a selected state. Click any state on the map to view its trajectories. Historical season context lines can be toggled on for comparison.

Data Sources

Hospitalization data is sourced from the CDC's National Healthcare Safety Network (NHSN). Forecasts are produced by the EpyStrain ensemble model.
